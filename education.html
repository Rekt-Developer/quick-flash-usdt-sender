Blockchain Education

What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a decentralized, distributed ledger technology that records transactions across many computers securely and transparently.

What is USDT?

USDT (Tether) is a stablecoin cryptocurrency that aims to maintain a value equal to the US dollar, providing stability in the volatile crypto market.

Safety Tips

  • Never share your private keys or recovery phrases
  • Always double-check recipient addresses before sending funds
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts and fake websites
  • Use hardware wallets for storing large amounts of cryptocurrency
  • Keep your software and firmware updated to patch security vulnerabilities

Blockchain Glossary

Block
A collection of transactions recorded and verified on the blockchain.
Smart Contract
Self-executing contracts with the terms directly written into code on the blockchain.
Gas
The fee required to conduct a transaction or execute a contract on the Ethereum blockchain.
Wallet
A digital tool that stores cryptocurrency and allows users to send and receive funds.
